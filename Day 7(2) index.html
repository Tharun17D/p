Konidela Pawan Kalyan

is a prominent Indian actor and politician known for his work primarily in Telugu cinema. Born on September 2, 1971, he is the younger brother of renowned actor Chiranjeevi. Pawan Kalyan gained fame for his roles in films such as *Tholi Prema*, *Gabbar Singh*, and *Kushi*, showcasing his versatility and earning a strong fan following. In addition to his film career, Pawan Kalyan is the founder of the Jana Sena Party, which he established in 2014 with the aim of addressing various social and political issues in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. His contributions to both cinema and politics have made him a significant public figure in South India.
